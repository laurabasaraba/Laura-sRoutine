My skincare routine!

Routine:
Added by:

Every morning I started to clean my face with a special cleaning gel and a small device to remove all impurities. After my face is clean I apply a cream containing vitamin C, hyaluronic acid, and at the end I apply spf to protect myself from the sun's rays. Thus, throughout the whole day, my face feels hydrated.
